Burguer

Description

A burguer is a grilled ground beef patty in between a bun

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Mayo, and ketchup
  • Hamburguer bun
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Grill the beef patty
  2. After the beef, insert the cheese on top of the grilled patty and grill the buns
  3. In a bowl stir the mayo and ketchup
  4. Make the burguer in this order:
    1. Bottom bun
    2. Sauce
    3. Patty with the cheese
    4. Sliced tomato
    5. Lettuce
    6. Sauce on the top bun and close the burguer
  5. Serve with a side of french fries with the sauce
