Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta plate, a very simple and fast recipe

From italy and made with very few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Fresh pasta
  • Bacon
  • Egg yolks
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon until crispy
  2. Boil the pasta with salt
  3. In a bowl, beat the egg yolks with the cheese and some pepper
  4. When the bacon is done add the pasta
  5. Remove from the heat and pour in the eggs while stirring, add some pasta water to make the sauce silky and smooth
  6. Be careful not to scramble the egg, make sure the pan is hot enough to cook it, but not to hot to scramble it
