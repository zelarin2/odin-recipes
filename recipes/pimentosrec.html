Pimentos Recheados

Description

Pimentos Recheados is a dish where u fill an entire bell pepper normally with meat

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Chorizo
  • Bacon
  • Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the onion with the garlic and a bay leaf
  2. After the onion has a good colour, add all the meat
  3. Season the meat with cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper
  4. Take the seeds out of the bell peppers to make it hollow
  5. Put the bell peppers in a baking tray and fill them up, insert the cheese on top
  6. Insert in the oven until the peppers are cooked through and the cheese is melted
  7. Serve preferably with rice or mashed potatoes
